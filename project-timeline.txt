## 🧠 Data Pipelines & Integrations

### **Distributed ETL Pipeline Architecture**
Designed and implemented a **scalable, fault-tolerant ETL framework** orchestrating data ingestion across multiple heterogeneous systems:

- Onsite Companion (operational data ingestion)
- Plexa (procurement and supply chain data)
- SimPro (field service management)
- BrightFox (CRM / contact intelligence)

The pipeline leverages:
- Stream-based processing
- Schema validation (runtime-safe)
- Data normalization and enrichment layers

Ensuring reliable delivery to downstream services with high observability and resilience.
